What does [Verified] mean?

If a Hex has the [Verified] label, that means the Hex is verified by Hexagon developers and meets the requirements for the Verification Program. The first Verified Hex was created by user "GetVerified" on October 29th, 2022. The account, "GetVerified", is an official developer account that is used to verify user-made Hexes.
